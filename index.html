Important Limitations & Considerations

While Co-Skip is powerful, be aware of potential limitations:

  • Screenshot Blocking:
    If you get blank screenshots (Ctrl+J), then use manual text input (Ctrl+L) as the primary method for your questions.
  • Hotkey Conflicts:
    Default hotkeys might interfere with other software or be blocked. **Always customize your hotkeys** (Ctrl+Shift+S) if defaults don't work.
  • Overlay Placement:
    Be sure to hover your mouse over the overlay BEFORE moving it (Ctrl+Shift+B). It is best to do this BEFORE starting any other program.
  • Responsible Use:
    You are solely responsible for adhering to all academic integrity policies, terms of service, and ethical guidelines. Misuse is strictly prohibited.

Test functionality beforehand if possible. Text-based input remains the most universally compatible feature.
