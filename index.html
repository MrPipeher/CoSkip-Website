Important Limitations & Considerations

While Co-Skip is powerful, be aware of potential limitations:

  • Screenshot Blocking:
    Highly secure environments may block screen capture (Ctrl+J). In these cases, rely on manual text input (Ctrl+L) as the primary method.
  • Hotkey Conflicts:
    Default hotkeys might interfere with other software or be blocked. **Always customize your hotkeys** (Ctrl+Shift+S) if defaults don't work. Extreme security might block all combinations.
  • Physical Visibility:
    Proctoring cameras can still see you physically typing or looking away. Maintain natural behavior.
  • Responsible Use:
    You are solely responsible for adhering to all academic integrity policies, terms of service, and ethical guidelines. Misuse is strictly prohibited.

Test functionality in a similar environment beforehand if possible. Text-based input remains the most universally compatible feature.
