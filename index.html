Two Ways to Conquer

Co-Skip adapts to your environment. Choose between absolute control or total discretion.

Method 1: The Standard Workflow

Best For: Complex questions, math problems, or adding specific instructions.

  • Capture (Ctrl + J) Press to take a full-screen screenshot. You can press this up to 5 times to combine multiple images into one complex question.
  • Add Text (Ctrl + L) Optional. Open the input bar to type specific instructions like "Solve for X" or "Summarize the last paragraph."
  • Send (Ctrl + M) Instantly bundles your images and text and sends them to the AI for processing.

Method 2: The Gesture Workflow

Best For: Speed and ergonomics. A completely keyboard-free experience.

  • The Trigger Simply press and hold your Right Mouse Button to activate the overlay.
  • The Capture Release the button. Co-Skip automatically processes the content on screen.
  • The Result The rationale appears instantly. Keep your hand on the mouse to maintain your flow state.
