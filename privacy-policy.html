Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 21, 2025

Welcome to Co-Skip. We are committed to a "Privacy-First" approach, ensuring that your data is handled with the highest level of discretion. This policy outlines how we handle limited technical data to provide our services and protect our intellectual property.

1. Information We Collect for Security & Authentication

While we aim to maintain user anonymity, we collect specific technical identifiers to fulfill our contract with you and ensure the security of our software:

  • Hardware Identifiers (HWID): Upon activation, our software generates a unique, non-reversible cryptographic hash of your hardware components. This is used strictly to link your license key to your device and prevent unauthorized account sharing.
  • IP Addresses: We log IP addresses during authentication and service usage. This is used for geographic licensing compliance, fraud prevention, and to maintain the integrity of our server infrastructure.
  • Security Logs: Our proprietary security system monitors for the presence of unauthorized investigative tools, debuggers, or virtualized environments. If a security violation is detected, technical data (including HWID and IP) is logged to enforce a permanent service ban.

2. Payment Processing

Purchases are handled by secure third-party providers (Stripe, Coinbase). While we do not store your full financial details on our own servers, these providers share limited transaction data (such as name and email) with us for order fulfillment and fraud prevention purposes. Cryptocurrency transactions are processed as final and offer the highest level of user anonymity.

3. AI Feature Interaction & Data Processing

When you utilize AI-powered features in Co-Skip, the captured data (text or screenshots) is transmitted securely to our processing infrastructure or third-party AI providers (such as Google Gemini or OpenAI).

  • No Permanent Storage: We do not log or store the content of your captures or the AI responses on our permanent databases. Data is processed in volatile memory and discarded once the response is delivered.
  • No PII Linking: Your capture data is never linked to your identity, hardware ID, or payment profile during the processing phase.

4. Information We Do *Not* Collect

We remain committed to avoiding unnecessary data collection:

  • We do not collect or store your browsing history.
  • We do not access or store your files, documents, or personal data outside of the explicit regions you choose to capture for AI analysis.
  • We do not sell your technical data or usage patterns to any third parties.

5. Website Interaction & Tracking

Our website (co-skip.com) uses standard tracking technologies (Cookies and Pixels) from Google, TikTok, Meta, and Reddit. This helps us understand our marketing reach and website performance. This website data is aggregated and is never linked to individual software license keys or hardware IDs.

6. Data Security

We utilize industry-standard encryption and kernel-level security measures to protect the integrity of the data we handle. In the event of a security breach attempt on our software, we reserve the right to use collected technical identifiers to protect our Service.

7. Key Handling & Support

Your license key is your primary identifier. Because we do not require user accounts, it is your sole responsibility to store your key. If you lose your key, our ability to recover it is limited and may require you to provide proof of transaction from your payment provider.

8. Contact Us

For any questions regarding your privacy, please contact our support team via our official Discord server.
