Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 21, 2025

Welcome to Co-Skip. Your privacy and anonymity are fundamental principles upon which Co-Skip is built. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning information – specifically, what we deliberately *do not* collect and what limited, non-personal data might be involved when you interact with our website or service.

Our Core Privacy Commitment: Zero User Data Collection

Co-Skip is designed from the ground up to operate without collecting or storing personally identifiable information (PII) about its users. We believe strongly in user anonymity.

  • No Personal Information Required: We do NOT require, request, collect, or store any personal details such as your name, email address, physical address, phone number, or any other direct identifiers to use Co-Skip.
  • Anonymous Payments via Stripe: Purchases are processed through Stripe's secure checkout pages. We do not receive or store your credit card details, billing address, or any other sensitive payment information. Stripe handles this independently according to their privacy policy. From our perspective, the transaction is anonymous – we only receive confirmation that a payment for a specific key type was successful.
  • Key System Enhances Privacy: We utilize a license key system specifically to avoid user accounts, logins, and the associated data collection they entail. Your key is your proof of purchase and access.
  • No Discord Linking: Your identity and activity within our Discord community server are entirely separate from your use of the Co-Skip software and your license key. We do not link Discord usernames to license keys.

Information We Do *Not* Store or Access

  • We do not maintain databases linking users to keys.
  • We do not store records of who purchased which specific key.
  • We do not share any user information with third parties (because we don't collect it in the first place, beyond the unavoidable interactions described below).

Website Interaction & Tracking Technologies

While the Co-Skip application itself operates anonymously, interacting with our website (co-skip.com) involves standard web technologies, including tracking pixels and cookies, used for website analytics and advertising purposes.

  • What are Pixels & Cookies? These are small pieces of code (pixels) or text files (cookies) placed on your device when you visit our website.
  • Why We Use Them:
    • To understand how visitors use our website (e.g., which pages are popular).
    • To measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns (e.g., counting clicks from ads).
    • To track conversions (purchases) that originate from our ads.
  • Who Provides Them & What They Collect: We use standard tracking technologies provided by Google, TikTok, and Meta. These services collect data like your IP address, browser type, and pages visited to help us analyze traffic and measure ad performance. Important: Co-Skip does not attempt to link this website browsing data to individual Co-Skip software users or license keys.

AI Feature Interaction

When you use features within Co-Skip that involve sending screenshots or text to an Artificial Intelligence (AI) model, that data is sent directly from your device to the AI service provider's servers. This interaction does *not* pass through Co-Skip's servers, and we do not log or store the content you send or the responses you receive.

Anonymous Usage & Key Management

To operate our service, we track usage data in a strictly anonymous manner. For plans with a question limit, our system maintains a record of each key's usage count. For "Unlimited" plans, we anonymously monitor usage patterns to ensure compliance with our Fair Use Policy and prevent service abuse.

This usage data is always anonymous and is never linked to any personal information.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect the integrity and security of the limited operational data we handle.

Key Handling & Recovery

Your unique license key is displayed on the Stripe confirmation page after payment. It is your sole responsibility to copy and securely store this key. As part of our commitment to anonymity, we do not store your personal purchase information linked to your license key. If you lose your key, you may contact support via Discord. By providing transaction details, we may be able to manually locate your key, but this is not guaranteed.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed towards individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, please reach out to us via our official Discord server support channels.
